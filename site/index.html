How it works

1

One seed

On install, Burner rolls a random master seed. It never leaves your machine — back up 32 characters and you can rebuild every identity anywhere.

2

Identities are math

SHA-256(seed : site) drives every field. Nothing is stored, nothing is synced — personas are derived fresh each time, identically.

3

One click

Names, emails, usernames, passwords, phones, birthdays — filled in one click, confirm-fields matched, checkboxes left alone.

Know who leaked your email

Three email modes. The tagged ones still receive mail — and when spam arrives on you+netflix.k3f@gmail.com, you know exactly who sold you out. The Sites tab keeps the receipts.

you+netflix.k3f@gmail.com

Install (30 seconds)

  1. Download the repo and unzip it (or git clone)
  2. Open chrome://extensions, flip on Developer mode
  3. Load unpacked → pick the extension/ folder
  4. Pin the flame. Open any signup page. Click. Done.
